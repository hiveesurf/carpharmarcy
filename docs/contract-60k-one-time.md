## 1. Purpose

The Service Provider agrees to design, develop, deliver, and support a web-based software solution for the Client under a fixed one-time commercial amount of **INR 60,000 (Rupees Sixty Thousand only)**, with **one (1) year handling/support** from production go-live.

---

## 2. Commercial Offer (Fixed Price)

- **Total Contract Value:** INR 60,000 (one-time, fixed)
- **Model:** Service-only (development + implementation + one-year handling/support)
- **Included:** Application development services, testing, deployment support, quarterly production release handling, basic operations assistance
- **Not Included (Billed Separately if required):**
  - Cloud hosting/infrastructure charges
  - Domain, DNS, SSL certificates
  - Third-party paid APIs, payment gateway fees, SMS/email provider fees
  - Any external licenses/subscriptions

All amounts are exclusive of applicable taxes, unless otherwise stated in invoice.

---

## 3. Scope of Work

The Service Provider will deliver:

1. Functional implementation of agreed modules/features
2. Secure backend and API integration
3. Payment/order/cart workflow implementation and hardening
4. Test coverage for key flows and bug-fix support
5. Production deployment support on agreed timeline
6. One-year handling/support (as defined in Section 8)

## 5. Delivery and Deployment Plan

The project follows a quarterly production deployment cadence:

- **Quarter 1:** Initial production launch

- **Quarter 3:** Planned production update/release
- **Quarter 4:** Planned production update/release and annual closure review

Deployment windows will be mutually scheduled with at least 5 business days prior notice.

## 6. Payment Terms

Suggested payment schedule:

- **40% (INR 24,000):** On contract sign-off and project kickoff
- **30% (INR 18,000):** On completion of core implementation milestone
- **20% (INR 12,000):** At initial production go-live

## 7. Acceptance Criteria

A phase is considered accepted when:

1. Agreed deliverables are demonstrated
2. Critical/blocker defects are resolved
3. Client approval is shared in writing (email/official chat/written sign-off)

If no written rejection is provided within 5 business days of delivery demo, the milestone shall be deemed accepted.

---

## 8. One-Year Handling/Support Terms

Support duration: **12 months from initial production go-live date**.

### 8.1 Included Support

- Minor bug fixes and stability improvements
- Production issue triage and guidance
- Quarterly release handling support
- Configuration-level support for existing modules

### 8.2 Response Targets (Business Hours)

- **Critical (prod down/security incident):** Initial response within 4 hours
- **High (major feature impacted):** Initial response within 1 business day
- **Medium/Low:** Initial response within 2 business days

### 8.3 Excluded from Included Support

- New major feature development beyond agreed scope
- Full redesign/re-architecture requests
- Third-party outages or vendor-side disruptions
- New compliance/legal implementations not in original scope

Excluded work can be handled via separate change request/commercial quote.

---

## 9. Change Request (CR) Policy

Any requirement that exceeds the documented scope will be treated as a Change Request and may impact:

- Cost

- Resource allocation

CR must be approved in writing by both Parties before implementation.

Client remains responsible for compliance obligations specific to their industry unless expressly contracted.
